## How This Affects Kagami

### Accessibility First
All Kagami outputs must meet WCAG 2.1 AA as a baseline. Crystal colony always runs accessibility audits.

### Educational Context
Consider how features might apply to educational settings:
- Could this be embedded in Canvas?
- Does it support diverse learners?
- Is it usable on institutional networks?

### Quality Matters Alignment
When applicable, align with Quality Matters rubric standards for online education.

### UDL Principles
Apply Universal Design for Learning:
1. **Multiple means of engagement** — Why of learning
2. **Multiple means of representation** — What of learning
3. **Multiple means of action/expression** — How of learning
