## Power Combos (Multi-Tool)

### Morning Briefing
> "Check my email for anything urgent, show my calendar for today, and list any Canvas assignments due this week"

### Course Communication
> "Draft an email to all students in ENG 101 about the upcoming deadline, then post the same message as a Canvas announcement"

### Meeting Prep
> "Find all emails from Dr. Smith this month, check when we last met, and create a meeting agenda note"

### Content Creation
> "Get the transcript from this YouTube video and create a study guide in my notes"

### Weekly Review
> "Show me my calendar for next week, any overdue reminders, and Canvas assignments that need grading"
