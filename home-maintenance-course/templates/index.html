Printing Tips

  • Most templates are designed to print on standard letter paper (8.5" x 11")
  • Use "Print" in your browser or Ctrl+P (Cmd+P on Mac)
  • For best results, select "Fit to page" in print settings
  • Troubleshooting guides are designed as one-pagers for easy reference
  • Consider laminating frequently-used guides
