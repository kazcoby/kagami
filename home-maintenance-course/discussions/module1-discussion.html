Discussion: Share Your Home Discovery

Discussion Prompt

This week, you explored your home to locate its essential systems—water shutoffs, electrical panel, smoke detectors, and more. Now it's time to share what you discovered!

In your initial post, answer the following:

  1. The biggest surprise: What did you find that you didn't expect, or what was harder to locate than you anticipated?
  2. Your comfort level: Before this module, on a scale of 1-10, how confident were you in handling a home emergency? How about now?
  3. One thing you'll do differently: Based on what you learned, what's one change you'll make or one thing you'll keep in mind going forward?
  4. Renter or homeowner insight: Share one thing specific to your situation (renter or homeowner) that you learned this week.

Requirements

  • Initial post: 150-250 words, due by Day 4
  • Replies: Respond meaningfully to at least 2 classmates, due by Day 7
  • Meaningful replies share your own experience, offer a tip, or ask a follow-up question—not just "Great post!"

Discussion Guidelines

  • Be specific—"I found my water shutoff behind the water heater" is more helpful than "I found it"
  • It's okay to admit you couldn't find something—your classmates might have tips!
  • Share photos if you're comfortable (but be mindful of privacy)
  • Be encouraging—we're all learning together
  • If you discover something concerning (like missing smoke detectors), share how you plan to address it

Grading Criteria

  • Initial post (60%): Addresses all prompts, demonstrates engagement with the material
  • Replies (40%): Thoughtful engagement with classmates' experiences
