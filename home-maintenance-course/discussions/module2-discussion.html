Discussion: Your Biggest Energy User

Discussion Prompt

This week we explored HVAC, appliances, and energy efficiency. Now let's share what we learned about our own homes!

In your initial post, address the following:

  1. Filter findings: Where is your HVAC filter located? What size is it? Was it dirtier than you expected?
  2. Appliance age check: How old are your major appliances (fridge, washer, dryer)? Any that might be energy hogs?
  3. One change you'll make: Based on this week's content, what's one efficiency improvement you plan to implement?
  4. Share a tip: Did you learn something not covered in the lessons? Found a helpful YouTube video? Share it with the class!

Requirements

  • Initial post: 150-250 words, due by Day 4
  • Replies: Respond meaningfully to at least 2 classmates, due by Day 7
  • Meaningful replies share your own experience, suggest additional tips, or ask follow-up questions

Discussion Guidelines

  • Be specific about your findings—dimensions, ages, conditions
  • If you discovered a problem (like a very old appliance), share your plan to address it
  • Feel free to share photos of particularly dirty filters (we've all been there!)
  • Regional tips are welcome—what works in Arizona may differ from Minnesota

Grading Criteria

  • Initial post (60%): Addresses all prompts, demonstrates engagement with your own home
  • Replies (40%): Thoughtful engagement with classmates' experiences and tips
