Basic Home Maintenance for Renters (and New Homeowners)

Welcome! Whether you're renting your first apartment or just bought your first home, this course will teach you the essential skills to keep your living space safe, comfortable, and in good condition.

Course Description

This 4-week noncredit course covers the fundamentals of home maintenance that every adult should know. You'll learn to identify potential problems before they become emergencies, perform simple repairs safely, and know when to call a professional.

No prior experience required! We assume you're starting from scratch and build your knowledge step by step.

What You'll Learn

  • How to locate and operate your home's essential systems (water shutoffs, electrical panel, HVAC)
  • Basic safety practices and emergency procedures
  • How to maintain appliances for longer life and lower bills
  • Simple plumbing fixes you can do yourself
  • When to DIY and when to call a professional
  • How to communicate effectively with landlords, HOAs, and contractors
Safety First! This course focuses on safe, beginner-appropriate tasks. We will NOT cover:
  • Gas line work of any kind
  • Electrical panel or wiring modifications
  • Major plumbing repairs
  • Roof work or ladder use beyond basic safety
  • Mold remediation (beyond identification and reporting)

When in doubt, we'll always tell you: "Stop and call a pro!"

Course Modules

Week 1: Home Orientation + Safety

Get to know your home's vital systems and learn essential safety procedures. By the end of this week, you'll be able to shut off water, reset circuit breakers, and respond to common emergencies.

Week 2: Air, Appliances + Efficiency

Learn to maintain your HVAC system, care for major appliances, and improve your home's energy efficiency. Small actions that save money and extend equipment life.

Week 3: Water + Moisture

Understand your plumbing system, prevent water damage, and tackle simple fixes like running toilets and slow drains. Plus: humidity control and leak detection.

Week 4: Simple Repairs + Your Maintenance System

Build your DIY toolkit, learn basic repairs, and create a personalized maintenance calendar. Graduate with a system to keep your home in great shape year-round.
