Module 1: Home Orientation + Safety

Estimated time: 2-3 hours

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Locate and identify your home's main utility shutoffs (water, gas, electrical)
  • Safely reset a tripped circuit breaker
  • Test and maintain smoke detectors and carbon monoxide alarms
  • Create an emergency contact list for your home
  • Identify which safety tasks are your responsibility vs. your landlord's
  • Recognize situations that require professional help

Why This Matters

Imagine water is gushing from under your kitchen sink at 11 PM. Do you know where to shut it off? What if your power goes out but your neighbor's lights are still on? These aren't hypothetical situations—they happen to almost everyone eventually.

This module is about knowing your home before an emergency happens. Taking an hour now to locate shutoffs and understand your systems can save you thousands of dollars and enormous stress later.

Pro Tip: As you go through this module, physically walk through your home and locate each system we discuss. Don't just read about it—find it!
