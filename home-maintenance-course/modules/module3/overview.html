Module 3: Water + Moisture

Estimated time: 2-3 hours

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Understand how your plumbing system works
  • Fix a running toilet (the most common plumbing issue)
  • Clear a slow or clogged drain safely
  • Reset a GFCI outlet (bathroom/kitchen safety device)
  • Identify signs of water damage and leaks
  • Control humidity to prevent mold growth
  • Know when plumbing issues require a professional

Why This Matters

Water damage is the second most common home insurance claim. A small leak can waste thousands of gallons of water yearly AND cause structural damage, mold growth, and expensive repairs.

The good news? Many common water issues—running toilets, slow drains, humidity problems—are easy fixes that anyone can handle.

What We WON'T Cover:
  • Main sewer line repairs
  • Water heater installation
  • Pipe replacement or soldering
  • Anything requiring cutting into walls
  • Mold remediation (beyond identify and report)

These require licensed professionals!

Materials Needed

  • Flashlight
  • Food coloring (for toilet leak test)
  • Plunger (cup style for sinks, flange style for toilets)
  • Access to under-sink areas
  • Phone/camera for documentation
Safety Reminder: Never mix drain cleaning chemicals. Never use chemical drain cleaners in standing water. When in doubt, call a plumber!
