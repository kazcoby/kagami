Module 2: Air, Appliances + Efficiency

Estimated time: 2-3 hours

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Locate and replace HVAC air filters
  • Understand basic thermostat programming for efficiency
  • Perform routine maintenance on major appliances
  • Identify signs that appliances need professional service
  • Implement simple energy-saving practices
  • Know when to DIY vs. call for appliance repair

Why This Matters

Your HVAC system and appliances are the workhorses of your home. A few minutes of regular maintenance can save hundreds in repair costs, extend equipment life by years, and significantly reduce your utility bills.

Plus, understanding how these systems work makes you a better advocate when dealing with repair technicians or landlords.

Money-Saving Fact: A dirty HVAC filter can increase your energy bills by 5-15%. Simply changing it regularly is one of the highest-ROI maintenance tasks you can do!
