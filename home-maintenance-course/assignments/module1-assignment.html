Assignment: Home Systems Photo Documentation

Overview

This hands-on assignment asks you to physically explore your home and document the location of critical systems. You'll create a reference that could be life-saving in an emergency and helpful for anyone who might need to assist you (house sitter, family member, etc.).

Time Required: 30-60 minutes to explore and document, plus time to compile your submission.

Instructions

Part 1: Photo Documentation (60 points)

Locate and photograph the following items in your home. For each photo, include a brief caption explaining what it is and exactly where it's located.

Required photos (10 points each):

  1. Main water shutoff valve — Include its location (e.g., "Basement, east wall near water heater")
  2. Electrical panel — Show the panel door closed AND open (can be two photos)
  3. One smoke detector — Note which room and if you tested it
  4. One CO detector (or combo unit) — Note location
  5. Water heater — Show the unit and its shutoff valve
  6. HVAC filter location — Show where the filter goes (furnace or return vent)
Can't Find Something? That's actually important data! If you can't locate an item, write a brief explanation of where you looked and what your next steps are (ask landlord, schedule inspection, etc.).

Part 2: Home Systems Checklist (20 points)

Download and complete the Home Systems Checklist from the Resources module. Fill in:

  • Locations of all shutoffs and systems
  • Emergency contact numbers (utilities, landlord if applicable)
  • Any issues or concerns you noticed

Upload the completed checklist with your submission.

Part 3: Reflection (20 points)

Write a brief reflection (100-150 words) addressing:

  • What was easiest to find? What was most challenging?
  • Did you discover any potential safety concerns? How will you address them?
  • How has this exercise changed your awareness of your home?

Submission Requirements

  • Submit all photos in a single document (PDF, Word) OR as a collection of image files
  • Include captions/labels for each photo
  • Attach your completed Home Systems Checklist
  • Include your written reflection

Grading Rubric

Criteria Excellent (100%) Satisfactory (75%) Needs Improvement (50%) Points
Photo Documentation All 6 items photographed clearly with detailed captions explaining exact locations 4-5 items photographed with adequate captions Fewer than 4 items, unclear photos, or missing captions 60
Checklist Completion All sections completed thoroughly with accurate information Most sections completed with minor gaps Significant sections incomplete or inaccurate 20
Reflection Thoughtful reflection addressing all prompts, shows genuine engagement with the material Addresses prompts but lacks depth or specificity Superficial or doesn't address the prompts 20

Total Points: 100

Privacy Note

Your photos are only viewed by your instructor. Be mindful not to include identifying information you're uncomfortable sharing (like your address on a piece of mail). Focus on the systems, not personal belongings.
